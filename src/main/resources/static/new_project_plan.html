Timeline
The Jogmodi IVD was started in the year of 2021 by Janki Foundation's co-founder's Mr and Mrs Mahesh Navani. Jogmodi village is a central hub for about 30 villages around it and hence it was selected as the anchor for this IVD. As part of this IVD, the founders of Janki Foundation wanted to work on 3 main causes to make the village more self suffient 1) A skill development centers like Taloring classes, Computer training institutes etc 2) Coaching the villagers on Organic Farming using new farming practices 3) Health and Nutrition awareness through our Arogya Sevikas.
